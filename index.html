The Challenge

The Growing Burden of Lifestyle Diseases

Modern lifestyles have led to a rise in silent health conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, and heart disease, often developing without noticeable symptoms. Without regular screening, these risks remain undetected until they become serious. In today's fast-paced world, lack of awareness and delayed diagnosis continue to impact long-term health outcomes and overall quality of life.

What We Offer

An Overview of Our Services

MEDCORE+ provides an efficient health screening experience that quickly offers a comprehensive overview of key health indicators. The service focuses on early detection of lifestyle-related conditions like cardiovascular risks, diabetes, and hypertension.

❤️

Cardiovascular Screening

6-Lead ECG for heart rhythm analysis, blood pressure measurements, and pulse rate monitoring for comprehensive cardiac health assessment.

🩸

Metabolic Panel

Blood sugar, HbA1c, uric acid, and hemoglobin testing to detect diabetes and related metabolic conditions early and accurately.

🫁

Pulmonary & Neuro

Lung capacity testing and neuropathy screening to assess respiratory function and early signs of nerve damage.

Body Composition

Advanced body composition analysis with 58+ metrics including BMI, visceral fat, muscle mass, and hydration levels.

Vital Signs Monitoring

Health Reports

Instant, structured health reports with clear insights into all key indicators, enabling informed decisions and proactive care.
